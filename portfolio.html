Portfolio

These are the projects I worked on, I learned Photoshop, Stop Motion, and Premiere. I have also learned Illustrator in the past.

Photoshop

This was my first time using Adobe Photoshop. I created this collage for my Intro to Digital Media and Design class using stock photos as well as non-destructive editing.

Stop Motion

For this Stop Motion project, I used my phone camera to take photos of goldfish as I moved them one by one. I also used Adobe Photoshop to create an animation using the timeline feature.

Video Project

This was my first time using Adobe Premiere. In this Video Project, I used my phone camera, a tripod, and a gimble to show you the process of going through a miniature golf course. I then used Adobe Premiere to edit the videos together.

Illustrator

I have used Adobe Illustrator before in a class in high school, and I have used it to create logos for my old competitive cheerleading gym. I created this logo using the Line, Offset Path, and Shape Builder tools.
